About me

I'm Rumsfeld Obeng, the founding member of INFINITE SPACE where we put your dreams into realities through art

As a graphic and a UI/UX designer, I have a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of visual communication. I create visually stunning designs that captivate audiences and convey messages effectively.
With my camera as my tool, I capture moments that tell stories and evoke emotions through photography. I excel in composing shots and playing with lighting for impactful and memorable images.
In animation, I bring characters and objects to life through creative storytelling and techniques.
My animations are dynamic, engaging, and visually captivating.

As a videographer, I produce high-quality video content that documents events, creates
compelling narratives, and leaves a lasting impression.
With my diverse skill set and unwavering passion for creativity, I continuously push boundaries and explore new horizons in my artistic journey.
